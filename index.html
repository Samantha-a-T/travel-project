Why Bologna?

Bologna: Rich in Architecture and Food

Bologna has long been considered one of the best- if not the best- cities in Italy for those seeking out great food. Yearly, Bologna sees approximately 1.5 million tourists who visit the city in search of ancient architecture and fantastic food, of which Bologna does not disappoint.

Bologna is home to Europe's second largest historic centre, expanding over 350 acres. This historic centre is home to buildings that boast medieval, renaissance, and baroque architecture.
